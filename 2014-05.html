Learn to Row Day

Our first learn to row day this year will be Thursday, May 29 from 5 p.m. to 8 p.m. The event is open house style, so you can stop by anytime between 5 p.m. and 8 p.m. Learn to Row day takes approximately one hour. Learn to row day is free!

All members and juniors are encouraged to volunteer. This is a great opportunity to introduce your friends, family and co-workers to rowing!

Senior Project Donates New Speed Coach

Sydney Shea, senior at Wellspring Prep High School, has donated a new SpeedCoach GPS to the club for her senior project. The SpeedCoach is like an erg display for shells! Thanks to Landon for mentoring and to everyone who supported the fundraising activities like car wash, can/bottle drive, dance and craft sales.
