Car Wash

Sydney Shea is hosting a car wash fundraiser for her senior project. The proceeds of this fundraiser will be used to buy a used (new to us!) boat. If you have clean used rags/towels for this event, please bring to the boathouse. And of course, stop by and get your dirty car washed!

Masters Nationals Regatta Sponsorships

The 2014 USRowing Masters Nationals Championships will be held in Grand Rapids from August 14th to 17th. This will be the largest regatta that we've ever hosted.

New Club Mascot

April 1 - We are proud to introduce our new club mascot -- Crabbie! Crabbie will be a fixture at all of our regattas this year. Try-outs for the role of Crabbie will be held at the Dock-in on April 5th. Bring your best dance moves and see if you have what it takes to represent our club!
