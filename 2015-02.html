Spring Learn to Row Classes

We are pleased to bring back our popular Learn to Row classes this spring and summer! In 2015, we will offer eight beginning rowing courses, as well as intermediate level classes for those with a little bit of experience, including sculling and advanced sweep rowing technique.

Note: Member rates for classes will be available 4/6/15.

Winter Season for Juniors

The Junior winter season registration is open! Register here. The cost of the season ($225) will include a 1-year membership at Westside Fitness, as well as a weekly cardio class, a weekly yoga class, and two days of erg training each week. This will provide a very well-rounded conditioning program for the juniors.
