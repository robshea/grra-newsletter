St. Pat's Sprint for Recovery in Chicago

Grand Rapids Rowing is participating in the 4th annual St. Pat's Sprint for Recovery. This event is a fundraiser for our friends at Recovery On Water. ROW gives survivors of breast cancer the unique opportunity to interact, become active in their recovery, and gain support from fellow survivors.

Please consider a donation on one of our member's pages. Thanks for supporting breast cancer recovery!

St. Patrick's Day Parade in Grand Rapids

All adult and junior rowers are welcome to participate in our entry in the Grand Rapids St. Patrick's Day parade. Please wear club gear or colors; red, black and white.

We are meeting at 10:15 AM at the corner of Lyon and Ionia. Our entry is in the first half of the parade. Please bring donations of wrapped candy, which will be passed out during the parade.
