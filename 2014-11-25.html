Winter Season

Here are the current details that we have on the Winter season.

Location - Westside Fitness Center, 1618 Leonard St NW, Grand Rapids, MI 49504

Adults - Westside Fitness will be offering discounted rates for Adult GRRA Rowers as well as Junior Parents. Details are still being hashed out, so please stay tuned.

Juniors - By signing up for Winter Training, Juniors will receive the following:

  • 1 year membership that can be used anytime (not just for Crew fitness)
  • In addition to Ergs, there will be access to treadmills, ellipticals, exercise bikes, and stair steppers.
  • Muscle Building - Access to Nautilus as well as free weights.
  • Additional scheduled free classes where the Juniors can work out with a Westside Fitnesses Trainer.

The following dates will be clarfied in the next few weeks.

  • Winter Session start date and schedule
  • Openhouse at Westside Fitnesss for all GRRA members
