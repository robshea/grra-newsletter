Membership Renewal

All memberships renew and are due on April 1, 2015. Login to your account now at grrowing.org to make a payment online. Payments may also be made by check at Dock-in, on Saturday, April 4th. Your membership must be renewed in order to row.

Spring Learn to Row Classes

We are pleased to bring back our popular Learn to Row classes this Spring and Summer! In 2015, we will offer eight beginning rowing courses, as well as intermediate level classes for those with a little bit of experience, including sculling and advanced sweep rowing technique.

Member rates for classes will be available April 6.

St. Pat's Sprint for Recovery

The Chicago Rowing Foundation hosts the St. Pats Sprint as a fundraiser for our friends at Recovery On Water. Their annual regatta is on March 14. Members, contact Coach Dave if you are interested in racing at this event.

The GR Crew Boosters have planned an exciting family-friendly fundraising event, "Rock & Row", to benefit the junior rowing program. The event will feature live music by the rock band, 'The Discrepancy'. March 21 at 6pm.

Food, water and soda will be available for sale. There will also be a 50/50 raffle with the winner announced at the end of the event (must be present to win).
