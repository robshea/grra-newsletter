GR Forward - Grand River Plan

There's a downtown planning process happening right now. A significant part of the plan is an in-depth study of the Grand River corridor. GRRA has a seat on the steering committee. An open house for public participation is being held between 3 and 7 pm every Thursday, Friday, and Saturday from now until November 21. Participation in this study is THE SINGLE MOST EFFECTIVE WAY for GR Rowing members to advocate for our sport and its future on the section of the Grand River that we currently use. Public participation processes like this one ("GR Forward") are an excellent opportunity to have a say in future development. Heavy involvement and participation by our members will have a significant impact on the outcome of the plan and subsequent development. (I was a city planner in GR for nearly 7 years and am speaking from experience).

Please plan to stop by at least once between 3 and 7 pm every Thursday, Friday, and Saturday from now until November 21. This is your chance to make rowing a key part of the Grand River plan!

- Landon

Kids' Food Basket

As part of our give back to the community, we have scheduled two volunteer dates at Kids' Food Basket. Kids' Food Basket attacks childhood hunger to help young people learn and live well in our local community.

Dates we are scheduled to volunteer. We will need 30 people for each date, first come first serve:

  • Monday, November 10, 6:00pm-8:00pm (Sandwich Making)
  • Monday, December 8, 6:00pm-8:00pm (Sandwich Making)

Location: 2055 Oak Industrial Drive NE, Grand Rapids, MI 49505

This volunteer opportunity is open to adult rowers and their families as well as junior rowers and their families. Kids younger than middle school age will have an opportunity to color sack supper bags during our scheduled time. Please come and hang out with your fellow rowers for a worthwhile cause. Please contact tracylynnshea@yahoo.com to sign up.
