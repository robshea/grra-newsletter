Yard Signs

Grand Rapids Rowing yard signs are available for sale. Show your rowing pride with one in your yard! Signs are $15. Contact Deb Suttner debbysuttner@gmail.com or Bekah Suttner to order a sign.

Can/Bottle Drive

The Junior Boosters have an ongoing can and bottle drive. Leave your bagged cans and bottles at the boathouse for collection. We've raised $40 to date. Keep them coming and thanks for your support!

Car Wash

The Junior Boosters are seeking a location for a Summer Car Wash. The ideal location will have access to water (for hoses, not for rowing) and good car traffic. All other supplies will be provided by the boosters. If you have a location available, contact Tracy Shea at tracylynnshea@yahoo.com.
