Greetings!

As we approach Masters Nationals in August, there are tasks for which we still need some help. (Thank you to everyone who stepped up to be a volunteer leader. We will be in touch with all of you within the next month or so).

Fundraising coordinator

We are looking for someone to function as a touchstone for people seeking fundraising and donations (basically making sure we aren't approaching the same people/places, identifying sectors of the community we are overlooking, and collecting information and paperwork). More about fundraising below.

Reception Food

We will be hosting a reception Friday evening (08/15/14) at Riverside Park. We have a source for live music, but could really use someone who would like to take on the challenge of planning food for an elegant soiree for 300 people on a limited budget.

Print Program

A program for the Nationals needs to be created and printed. Any designers/publishers out there?

Signage

Some design work and an order for signs needs to be placed (we have the printing source already). Would you like to inform people and tell them where to go?

Batteries

We will require 144 batteries for use over the course of Nationals. Would anyone like to organize a battery drive?

Trailer

The club will be purchasing a new equipment trailer. Does anyone have any experience with or a good source for procuring a trailer? And would that person be interested in presenting options for purchase to the board?

Start Tower Scaffolding

We are having trouble finding a source that can build the scaffolding for the start tower. The big issue seems to be its placement in the water. We have a schematic, if you have a source in mind and would like to sound them out!
